Our Solution

Our solution provides real-time detection at both OSI Layer 4 (Transport) and Layer 7 (Application) levels, ensuring comprehensive protection. Powered by advanced machine learning algorithms, Packet Vision predicts potential threats, boasting high accuracy rates. Furthermore, our system offers actionable mitigation recommendations, enabling swift and effective responses to potential attacks, securing your network and maintaining operational integrity.

Technical Solution

Our technical innovation lies in our advanced data collection and machine learning models. We employ real-time packet capture and feature extraction, enabling time-windowed traffic pattern analysis and protocol-specific anomaly detection. Our ensemble approach combines Random Forest, LSTM, and Isolation Forest models, enhanced by transfer learning from historical attack patterns. Continuous model improvement ensures adaptability to emerging threats, providing robust and intelligent DDoS defense.

Data Collection & Preprocessing

  • Real-time packet capture
  • Feature extraction from network traffic
  • Time-windowed analysis
  • Protocol-specific pattern recognition

ML Models & Frameworks

  • Random Forest for classification
  • LSTM for sequence prediction
  • Isolation Forest for anomaly detection
  • Transfer learning from historical attack data
